PENELOPE MCSTOMPSALOT

From San Francisco artist and customizer Huck Gee’s toy treasure trove comes the Penelope McStompsalot Dunny: an 8-inch vinyl vessel, fitted with a 3-inch vinyl Dunny pilot. A marvel of vinyl engineering, this mechacreation is displayed in a window box designed by the artist.
